About

Henna is more than decoration — it's tradition, celebration, and self-expression. As a dedicated Mehndi artist, I specialize in creating unique designs for weddings, festivals, and personal occasions. From intricate bridal patterns to minimal modern styles, every design is hand-drawn with care and creativity. I believe in turning hands and hearts into canvases of art through intricate patterns and personalized designs.

Manpreet Kaur - Henna & Mehndi Artist

With years of experience and a love for tradition, I bring elegant designs to life for brides, families, and cultural events.

  • Experience: 5+ years in bridal and event mehndi
  • Based in: Ottawa, ON, Canada
  • Services: Bridal Mehndi, Party Mehndi, Festival Henna, Custom Designs
  • Available for: Weddings, Festivals, Events
  • Languages: English, Punjabi, Hindi

I believe each design tells a story. Whether simple or elaborate, I work closely with clients to ensure their vision is beautifully reflected in every detail.
